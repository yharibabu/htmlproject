LEGAL RIGHTS

1.Equal Pay: Laws ensuring that men and women are paid equally for the same work (e.g., Equal Pay Act).

2.Workplace Equality: Rights protecting against discrimination based on gender in hiring, promotions, and workplace treatment (e.g., Title VII of the Civil Rights Act).

3.Maternity and Paternity Leave: Rights for both parents to take time off after the birth or adoption of a child, without losing their job (e.g., Family and Medical Leave Act).

4.Protection Against Gender-Based Violence: Laws that protect against gender-based harassment, including domestic violence, sexual harassment, and stalking.

5.Education Equality: Rights that ensure equal access to education for all genders, preventing gender discrimination in schools and colleges (e.g., Title IX).

6.Property and Inheritance Rights: Ensuring that all genders have equal rights to own, inherit, and manage property.
